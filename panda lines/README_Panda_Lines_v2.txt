Panda Lines v2

Files:
- Panda Lines v2.ex4: compiled MT4 indicator
- Panda Lines v2.mq4: MT4 source
- Panda Lines v2.ex5: compiled MT5 indicator
- Panda Lines v2.mq5: MT5 source

Install:
1. In MetaTrader, open File > Open Data Folder.
2. Put the MT4 .ex4/.mq4 files in MQL4/Indicators.
3. Put the MT5 .ex5/.mq5 files in MQL5/Indicators.
4. Restart MetaTrader or right-click Navigator > Refresh.
5. Drag Panda Lines v2 from Navigator > Indicators onto your chart.
