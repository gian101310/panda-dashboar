TRADING STRATEGIES

BB — BREAKOUT BIAS
EntryGap ≥ 5 (valid bias), any time, any day. TBG NOT required.
ConcurrencyNo new entry if same pair has open BB trade. Re-entry allowed once closed.
ExitGap drops >2 points from peak gap value.
INTRA — INTRADAY
EntryGap ≥ 9 + TBG confirmed (ABOVE=BUY, BELOW=SELL). Window: 2AM-4AM UAE only.
ConcurrencySame as BB — no duplicate per pair.
Exit10AM UAE hard close — no exceptions.

GAP SCORING

GAP = BASE score - QUOTE score (D1 + H4 + H1)
BUY Gap ≥ 5    SELL Gap ≤ -5    WAIT Between
MARKET |gap| ≥ 9    PULLBACK |gap| ≥ 5
Range: -18 to +18

TBG ZONES

ABOVE
BUY valid
BELOW
SELL valid
BETWEEN
Always invalid

CONFIDENCE SCORING (Frontend, 0-100)

Gap Magnitude ~25-30 pts
TBG Valid +20 pts
Box Aligned +20 pts
COT +10 pts
Momentum +10 pts

MOMENTUM STATES

🔥 STRONG — RIDE IT. All 3 TF aligned.
🚀 BUILDING — ENTER NOW. Short + mid rising.
⚡ SPARK — WATCH. Early signal, not confirmed.
🔵 CONSOLIDATING — HOLD. Strong trend resting.
🌡️ COOLING — TIGHTEN SL. Slowing but intact.
📉 FADING — CONSIDER CLOSING. Gap shrinking.
⚠️ REVERSING — CLOSE POSITION. Trend breaking.
▬ STABLE — MONITOR. No strong momentum.
○ NEUTRAL — WAIT. Gap below ±5.
📈 EMERGING — PREPARE ENTRY. Signal emerging.
